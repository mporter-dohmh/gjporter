Singing and Praying Bands of Maryland and Delaware

Recorded at Crossroads Music April 2014

The Singing and Praying Bands maintain an unbroken tradition of worship originating among African American Methodists in the mid-Atlantic in the early 19th century. Methodist prayer meeting practice is joined to a ring shout, a blend of various West African traditions of song and circle dancing thought to have contributed to the creation of a unified African-American identity.
