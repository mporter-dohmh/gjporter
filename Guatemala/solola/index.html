Solola
June 2006
Videos by Judith Porter
Solola is the capital of the department of Solola an area that includes 19 municipalities around Lake Atitlan, Guatemala.  The majority (approximately 90%) of the city's inhabitants are Cakchiquel Mayans.  It is one of the few municipalities where both men and women routinely wear their traditional Mayan dress.
