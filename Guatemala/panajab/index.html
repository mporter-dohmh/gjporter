Panajab
June 2006
Videos by Judith Porter
Panajab is a small town on lake Atitlan in Guatemala.  In October 2005, Tropical Storm Stan's rains triggered a mudslide that buried Panajab,  leaving 600 -  1000 people feared dead.  In this clip we visit Panajab to see the destruction and to distribute some photos that a previous tourist had taken.
