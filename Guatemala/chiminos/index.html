Chiminos Island and Aguateca
June 2006
Videos by Judith Porter
Chimino's Island is located in Punta de Chimino, on the beautiful Petexbatún  Lagoon in the southeastern Peten, in Guatemala's jungle region.  Aguateca is a partly excavated Mayan site.  The video includes the trip through the lagoon, a jungle walk, some video of howler monkeys, a visit to Aguateca and a stop at a small village near Chiminos Lodge.
