Adele Honigwill's Shoah Testimony
January 14, 1997


Adele Honigwill is Judy Porter's aunt.  Her mother, father and two of her brothers were killed in the Holocaust.  Because she was young and strong she was taken to slave labor camps and eventually to Bergen-Belsen Concentration Camp. After the war she came to the US where her other brother, Judy's dad lived.  This is her story.
