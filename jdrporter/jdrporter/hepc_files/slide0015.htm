June 13, 2002
Community Epidemiology Work Group
CONCLUSIONS
nWidespread knowledge that HCV is a disease affecting drug injectors.
nSome have basic information that HCV affects the liver, is potentially fatal, and transmitted by syringe sharing. 25% have no correct information.
nThere is confusion and inaccuracy about types of hepatitis, transmission, symptoms, effects, prevention, testing.
nLess knowledge of HCV than of HIV; few knew if they were tested or knew their HCV status.
