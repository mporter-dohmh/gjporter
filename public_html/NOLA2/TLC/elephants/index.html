"Elephant Village" is a privately owned elephant camp and tour destination approved by the Laos government and operated by International specialists and volunteers who focus on protection and rehabilitation of elephants in Laos. The Elephant Village is in a lush jungle valley on the banks of the Nam Khan River,  15 kms. southeast of Luang Phabang in the ancient village of Xieng Lom.
