Submit Application
Once you have completed all of the above information, click on the 'Prepare Application' button below. This will send the application information to the APIC Secretary/Treasurer for processing. 
If you are paying by credit card, you will then see a button to actually enter your credit card information. 
If paying by check or money order, a completed application will appear on the screen for you to print and mail in with your payment. The printed version has the address of the APIC Secretary/Treasurer printed on the bottom. This is where your application and payment should be sent. 
If you have any questions regarding this application or the application process, please contact the APIC Webmaster or the APIC Secretary/Treasurer.
