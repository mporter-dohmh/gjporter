Papua New Guinea - August 2008
Judy and Jerry Porter

In August 2008 we traveled to Papua New Guinea.  On this trip we attended the Mt. Hagen Singsing, traveled on the Sepik River to visit villages, spent time in the Southern Highlands (the home of the Huli Wigmen), and went to Madang on the coast.  We flew in and out of Port Morsby.
These photos and videos are from this trip.

our trip was organized by Asia Transpacific Journeys
